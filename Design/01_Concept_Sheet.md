# Cat Ninja 3 — Concept Sheet

## Title
Cat Ninja 3

## Elevator Pitch
Cat Ninja 3 is a single-player browser-based HD-2D pixel-art precision action platformer where a mostly realistic house cat in ninja gear battles through exaggerated urban-domestic environments using heavy movement, mouse-aimed shuriken, precise claws, frenzy mode, and a time-charged hairball super.

## Genre
2D precision action platformer

## Platform
Desktop web browser

## Player Fantasy
The player is a stylish, deadly, house-cat action hero: agile but weighty, funny but cool, familiar but exaggerated into arcade combat fantasy.

## Setting
The world is inspired by places a real house cat would know or encounter:
- apartments
- living rooms
- kitchens
- hallways
- balconies
- malls
- office floors
- parking structures
- rooftops
- storefronts
- service corridors

Human spaces become traversal and combat architecture at cat scale.

## Tone
- Playful retro parody
- Cool arcade action
- Absurd urban-comedy
- Readable and punchy, not chaotic or random

## Protagonist
A mostly realistic house cat in ninja gear.

### Visual Intent
- Retains feline silhouette and body language
- Not heavily anthropomorphic
- Agile, expressive, poised, arrogant, athletic
- Ninja identity should enhance the cat, not replace it

## Core Gameplay
The player:
- moves through platforming spaces
- fights enemies with precision
- manages ammo and powerups
- uses dash and positioning to survive
- reaches checkpoints
- defeats a miniboss to clear the level

## Core Mechanics
### Movement
- Run
- Jump
- Double jump
- Wall jump
- Dash

### Combat
- Single precise claw strike
- Mouse-aimed shuriken
- Slow-motion aiming
- Dash collision damage
- Hairball super

### Powerups
- Catfood = health restore
- Catnip = frenzy mode charge

### Survival
- Health bar
- 9 lives per level
- Checkpoint respawn
- Short death animation

## Signature Mechanics
1. Mouse-aimed shuriken
2. Slow-motion aim mode
3. Nine-lives level structure
4. Heavy, committed movement
5. Time-charged hairball super

## Key Enemies
### Grandmothers
Ordinary grannies with absurd combat skills.

### Laser T-Rex
A ridiculous urban-commercial security/mascot-style miniboss.

## MVP Level
**Mall Mayhem**  
A mall-like urban-commercial interior that teaches movement, combat, ammo use, frenzy timing, and ends in a laser T-Rex boss fight in a mall atrium.

## What Makes This Game Distinct
- House-cat fantasy rather than generic ninja or generic mascot platformer
- Domestic and commercial environments reimagined at cat scale
- Mouse aiming in a 2D platformer
- Tone blending absurd comedy with genuinely satisfying action
- Familiar spaces turned into dramatic traversal arenas
